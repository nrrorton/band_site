About Us

We’re Nick and Nick — two guys with similar music tastes and a blend of their own unique individuality. We've started a band and want you all to come have a listen!

Nick #1: ex-nobody turned current overthinker with a “software engineering brain” and a stubborn desire to do something meaningful before the end. I'm rebuilding my life piece by piece, and life as a drummer plays a major part in this. So far, enjoying every bit of it and hoping to share a bit of this with those willing to lend me an ear or two.

Nick #2: Current musician and teacher of children who wants to be successful in every aspect of life. I'm constantly working to create the best sound possible for the people to hear. I am the younger 'Nick' who sings and plays guitar.

We play covers, write originals, and watch as others confuse us two given the shared name. If you catch a show, you’ll get a mix of grit, grunge, melody, swing, and other loads of musical fun and mischief.
